Our Mission & Our Advocacy

🏷️ Title of the Project

ENHANCING CYBERSECURITY FRAMEWORK FOR DIGITAL THREATS

By Group One PH  |  Fatima National High School, General Santos City  |  Project Lead: Janlei Gonzales

📖 Introduction

Our group's mission is to strengthen digital resilience by developing a structured and adaptive cybersecurity framework capable of addressing evolving digital threats. Our vision is to promote a secure digital environment where organizations can operate with minimized exposure to cyber risks while maintaining operational efficiency.

The project, Enhancing Cybersecurity Framework for Digital Threats, focuses on evaluating existing security controls, identifying vulnerabilities, and designing a layered defense model tailored to modern threat landscapes. As cyberattacks become more sophisticated — ranging from phishing and ransomware to insider threats — organizations require proactive and systematic security strategies rather than reactive solutions. This project aims to build a scalable framework aligned with industry best practices and risk management principles.

🎯 Purpose

This project is worth the time, effort, and financial support of sponsors because cybersecurity incidents can lead to financial losses, reputational damage, legal liabilities, and operational disruptions. Strengthening security infrastructure reduces exposure to these risks and improves stakeholder confidence.

The initiative provides measurable value by:

  • Conducting risk assessments to identify security gaps
  • Recommending cost-effective security controls
  • Improving incident response readiness
  • Promoting cybersecurity awareness within the organization

By investing in preventive security architecture rather than post-incident recovery, sponsors allocate resources toward long-term stability and sustainable digital growth.

📋 Description of the Project

The project involves five major phases, each building toward a complete, deployable cybersecurity framework:

PHASE 01
Assessment Phase
Evaluate current IT infrastructure, network architecture, and data protection practices.
PHASE 02
Threat Analysis
Identify potential digital threats relevant to the organization's industry and operations.
PHASE 03
Framework Development
Design a multi-layered framework incorporating access control, encryption, network monitoring, and incident response protocols.
PHASE 04
Implementation Plan
Provide step-by-step recommendations for deployment, including timelines and resource allocation.
PHASE 05
Training & Awareness
Develop basic cybersecurity awareness materials for staff to reduce human-related vulnerabilities.

The framework will be based on structured security principles such as risk management, defense-in-depth strategy, and continuous monitoring. Documentation, risk reports, and implementation guidelines will be delivered at project completion.
